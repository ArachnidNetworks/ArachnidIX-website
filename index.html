About


ArachnidIX is a free and neutral Internet Exchange Point to facilitate Arachind Network peering. Arachnid Networks AS207863 is also present at other IXPs. Please consult our PeeringDB page for more details.


We are an IPv6 only exchange. We utilize the following prefix(s) for the peering VLAN:

  • 2a0e:b107:290::/44

AS207863 has a selective peering policy, but is peered with the ArachnidIX route servers.



Connectivity


ArachnidIX is both free and open, and we are glad to welcome any new member with adequite reason for connection. ArachnidIX is a small exchange, and might not be right for everyone. Drop us an email at ix@arachnid.cc to inquire about joining the exchange.


We do not charge for ports or a membership fee. The exchangs is funded by your donations. If you have a few dollars, please consider making a donation. https://arachnid.cc/donate


Additionally, members must agree to the following terms:


  • Must have a public ASN and IPv6 space from an IANA recognized RIR. (IPv4 is not supported at this time.)
  • Must only use ARP, IP and IPv6 layer 2 protocols. Other protocols such as CDP, UDLD, EDP, VTP, DTP, LLDP and others must be disabled.
  • IPv6 router solicitation/DHCPv6 must be disabled.
  • Must not cause any intentional harm or disruption to others on the exchange or on the internet.
  • Must not point the default routes to another member unless given explicit permission.
  • Must have an accurate PeeringDB profile.
  • Must be able to establish connectivity to the exchange either through a physical connection or a tunnel.
  • Must establish a BGP session with our route servers. (Contact us if you would not like to peer with the route servers.)
  • If requesting a tunnel, you must provide adequite justification for why the tunnel would be benificial for both parties.
